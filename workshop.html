mHealth Security Workshop

Protecting mobile and connected health ecosystems through deployable security, privacy, policy, and interdisciplinary research.

What the workshop does

A focused forum for the full mHealth security lifecycle

Smartphone apps, wearables, implantables, remote monitoring, cloud analytics, and EHR integrations create new ways to deliver care—and new attack surfaces. This workshop brings researchers, clinicians, practitioners, developers, and policymakers together around practical defenses and open challenges.
